# Olist Retail Intelligence Project

## Objective
Analyze e-commerce performance focusing on revenue, delivery efficiency, and customer behavior.

## Data Cleaning Summary
- Converted timestamps to datetime
- Removed invalid prices
- Standardized ZIP codes
- Handled missing product categories

## Key Insights
- Certain states have significantly higher delivery delays
- Top sellers dominate specific product categories
- Credit card users have higher average order values
- High-value customers place repeat orders

## Business Recommendations
- Improve logistics in high-delay states
- Focus marketing on high-performing categories
- Incentivize high-value customers
